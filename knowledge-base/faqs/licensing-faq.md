# Licensing FAQ

## Overview

Frequently asked questions related to licenses used during CloudLabs lab environments.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why do I need a license?

Some Microsoft services require active licenses before features become available.

---

### What happens if a trial expires?

Features associated with the trial license may no longer be available.

---

### Which services commonly require licenses?

- Microsoft Fabric
- Power Automate
- Microsoft 365
- Azure AI services

---

### Can I activate a free trial?

Some services provide trial licenses depending on Microsoft eligibility.

---

### Why can't I create a flow in Power Automate?

A required Power Automate license may not be available.

---

### Why can't I access Microsoft Fabric features?

The workspace or tenant may not have the required Fabric capacity or permissions.

---

### Can licenses take time to activate?

Yes. Propagation may take several minutes.

---

### Do all labs require paid licenses?

No. Many labs provide temporary environments with the required licensing already configured.

---

### Can I use my personal Microsoft account?

Only if the lab instructions explicitly allow it.

---

### Who manages lab licenses?

CloudLabs provisions and manages licenses according to the lab configuration.

---

### Best Practices

- Use the account provided for the lab.
- Wait for license propagation after activation.
- Verify tenant selection before troubleshooting.
