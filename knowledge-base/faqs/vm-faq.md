# Virtual Machine (VM) FAQ

## Overview

This document answers common questions related to CloudLabs virtual machines used in hands-on lab environments.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Why is my VM taking a long time to start?

Provisioning a virtual machine may take several minutes depending on Azure resource availability. Wait 3–5 minutes before refreshing the lab.

---

### 2. Why do I see a black screen?

A temporary black screen may appear while the VM initializes. Refresh the browser if the issue persists.

---

### 3. Can I refresh the VM page?

Yes. Perform a hard refresh using:

- **Windows:** `Ctrl + Shift + R`
- **Mac:** `Cmd + Shift + R`

---

### 4. My VM disconnected. What should I do?

Reconnect to the lab. If the problem continues, restart the lab session.

---

### 5. Why is the VM slow?

Performance may be temporarily affected by Azure resource utilization or network latency.

---

### 6. Can I reboot the VM?

Only if instructed by the lab guide.

---

### 7. Can I resize the VM?

No. VM size is managed by the lab environment.

---

### 8. My VM is stuck on "Starting". What should I do?

Wait a few minutes, refresh the browser, and verify your internet connection.

---

### 9. Why can't I copy and paste?

Some browser or VM settings may temporarily restrict clipboard functionality.

---

### 10. Can I use Remote Desktop?

Only if the lab specifically requires it.

---

### 11. What browsers work best?

Microsoft Edge and Google Chrome are recommended.

---

### 12. What should I do if the VM never loads?

Review the troubleshooting guide and restart the lab if necessary.
