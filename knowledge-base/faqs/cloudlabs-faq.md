# CloudLabs Frequently Asked Questions

## Overview

This document answers common questions related to CloudLabs hands-on lab environments.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. What is CloudLabs?

CloudLabs is a cloud-based hands-on learning platform that provides pre-configured lab environments for Azure, Microsoft Fabric, Power Platform, AWS, and other cloud technologies.

---

### 2. Do I need an Azure subscription?

No. Most CloudLabs labs provide temporary Azure subscriptions as part of the lab environment.

---

### 3. Why is my VM taking a long time to start?

Virtual machine provisioning may take several minutes depending on Azure resource availability. Wait a few minutes before refreshing the page.

---

### 4. Which browsers are supported?

- Microsoft Edge (Latest)
- Google Chrome (Latest)

---

### 5. Can I use Safari or Firefox?

Some labs may not function correctly. Chrome or Edge is recommended.

---

### 6. Why can't I sign in?

Verify that you are using the credentials provided for the lab and not your personal Microsoft account.

---

### 7. Why is the Azure Portal not loading?

Refresh the browser, clear cache, or open the portal in an Incognito/InPrivate window.

---

### 8. Why is my deployment failing?

Check the deployment error details, verify permissions, and retry after a few minutes.

---

### 9. Can I restart my lab?

Yes. Restart the lab from the CloudLabs portal if necessary.

---

### 10. What happens if my session expires?

Restart the lab and sign in again using the provided credentials.

---

### 11. How long are lab sessions available?

The duration depends on the lab configuration assigned by the instructor.

---

### 12. Are my changes saved?

Changes remain available during the active lab session unless otherwise specified.

---

### 13. Can I pause a lab?

This depends on the lab configuration.

---

### 14. What if I accidentally delete a resource?

Restart the lab if resource recreation is not part of the exercise.

---

### 15. Where can I find troubleshooting guidance?

Refer to the Troubleshooting folder within the knowledge base.
