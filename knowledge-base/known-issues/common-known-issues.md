# Common Known Issues

## Overview

This document lists common issues that users may encounter while completing CloudLabs labs.

---

## Issue 1 – VM Provisioning Delay

### Symptoms

- VM remains on the Starting screen.
- Blank screen after launch.

### Workaround

Wait 3–5 minutes before refreshing.

### Status

Known behavior.

---

## Issue 2 – Azure Portal Loading Slowly

### Symptoms

- Azure Portal takes a long time to load.

### Workaround

Refresh the page or use an Incognito/InPrivate window.

### Status

Known behavior.

---

## Issue 3 – Browser Cache Issues

### Symptoms

- Buttons not responding.
- Blank pages.

### Workaround

Clear browser cache and cookies.

### Status

Resolved after browser refresh.

---

## Issue 4 – Authentication Timeout

### Symptoms

- Sign-in process loops repeatedly.

### Workaround

Sign out, close the browser, and sign in again.

### Status

Intermittent.

---

## Issue 5 – Temporary Azure Delays

### Symptoms

- Resource provisioning is delayed.

### Workaround

Retry after a few minutes.

### Status

Platform dependent.

---

## Issue 6 – Deployment Timeout

### Symptoms

- ARM deployment exceeds expected duration.

### Workaround

Retry the deployment after verifying parameters.

### Status

Known behavior.

---

## Issue 7 – Workspace Permissions

### Symptoms

- Access Denied.
- Unable to create resources.

### Workaround

Verify workspace role assignments.

### Status

Resolved after permission propagation.

---

## Issue 8 – Data Agent Missing

### Symptoms

- Data Agent option not available.

### Workaround

Verify tenant settings and workspace permissions.

### Status

Preview feature limitation.

---

## Issue 9 – Session Expiration

### Symptoms

- Unexpected sign-out.

### Workaround

Restart the lab session.

### Status

Expected behavior after timeout.

---

## Issue 10 – Slow Portal Performance

### Symptoms

- UI becomes sluggish.

### Workaround

Refresh the browser or restart the lab.

### Status

Intermittent.
