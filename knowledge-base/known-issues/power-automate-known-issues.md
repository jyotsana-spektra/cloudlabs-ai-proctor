# Power Automate Known Issues

## Overview

Known issues encountered while using Microsoft Power Automate during CloudLabs exercises.

---

## Issue 1 – Unable to Create Flow

### Symptoms

- Create button disabled.
- Flow creation fails.

### Resolution

Verify that a valid Power Automate license is assigned.

---

## Issue 2 – Trial Cannot Be Activated

### Symptoms

- Trial option unavailable.

### Resolution

Ensure the account is eligible for a Microsoft trial license.

---

## Issue 3 – Connector Authentication Failure

### Symptoms

- Unable to sign in to connectors.

### Resolution

Reconnect the connector and verify account permissions.

---

## Issue 4 – Flow Save Failure

### Symptoms

- Flow cannot be saved.

### Resolution

Check licensing, connection references, and required permissions.

---

## Issue 5 – Missing Templates

### Symptoms

- Templates do not appear.

### Resolution

Refresh the portal and verify the selected environment.

---

## Status

Most issues are related to licensing or connector configuration.
