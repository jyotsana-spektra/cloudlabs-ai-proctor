# Brainy (CloudLabs AI Proctor)

Real-time assistance during hands-on labs and live events. Answers attendee questions, explains lab steps, and troubleshoots issues — escalating to human support only when required.
- Self Paced Labs
- Instructor Led Labs
- Live Events

Instead of a learner waiting for a human proctor to respond in a live chat, Brainy gives an immediate, context-aware answer generated from CloudLabs' own knowledge base plus 

Azure OpenAI reasoning — with a safety net of general web search and, ultimately, a real support email when nothing else applies.

Live product name in the UI: "Brainy — AI Lab Copilot"

# The Problem We're Solving

Live labs create a support bottleneck. Every stuck attendee needs a human — proctors, L1/L2/L3 support, engineers pulled off core work.

## Business Value

- **Reduce proctor cost** — Cut dependency on paid lab proctors for routine assistance.
- **Deflect L1/L2/L3 tickets** — Resolve routine support requests instantly, before they escalate.
- **Better learner experience** — Instant, always-on AI help keeps attendees unblocked and moving.
- **Free up services team** — Let the services team focus on lab onboarding, testing, and quality.
- **Scale more labs** — Optimize operational cost and run more labs without adding headcount.

# CloudLabs Integration Vision

Brainy, integrated directly into the CloudLabs lab environment — a native experience, not a separate tool.

- **Native to the Lab Guide** — Brainy lives inside the lab environment — accessible from the guide without switching context.
- **Ask without leaving the lab** — Learners get answers in-place, keeping hands on the task and momentum intact.
- **Screen Awareness (future)** — Contextual guidance based on the learner's current lab step — proactive, not just reactive.

# What Brainy Handles for Attendees

The most common blockers in a live lab — resolved instantly, in context.

- **VM connectivity** — VM access, RDP, network and connectivity problems.
- **Login & portal issues** — Sign-in, auth, Azure, Power Apps, M365, Copilot logins.
- **Troubleshooting** — Errors and issues in lab steps and tasks.
- **Tips & best practices** — Useful recommendations and quick wins.
- **Anytime, if stuck** — Ask anything at any point in the lab.
- **Smart escalation** — Hands off to a human only when truly required.
